而夏天的文学意象又在各种意义上都是暧昧不明的——无尽鲜活的绿色、明媚冗长的白昼、闷热的晚风、不期而至的大雨、热烈短暂的亲密关系——意指积极的、限定美好的一切，好像没有理由不从夏天开始发生。

The literary image of summer is ambiguous in every sense - endless vibrant green, bright and long days, sultry evening breezes, unpredictable downpours, heated and fleeting intimacies - positive meaninglessness in everything - as if there's no reason why it won't happen from summer on.

Mother ties her apron, then a crisp sound is made on the wooden chopping board, and the round body of the melon breaks into small, eloquent triangles. Summer begins, tenderly, subtly, and with feminine meaning.

西瓜生理结构的演变历程昭示着它几乎可以被用作是当代女性主义的完美喻体。在完全自然的、去人类干预的状态下，西瓜原本是小小的，不那么甜，也没有那么多汁的果肉；籽倒是有很多，作为最重要的生殖器官，也被精心编码成不易消化的结构，作为其健康繁衍与发展的基石。

The evolution of the watermelon's physiology indicates that it could almost be used as a perfect metaphor for contemporary feminism. In a completely natural, dehumanized state, the watermelon was originally very small, less sweet, and less juicy. There were plenty of seeds, as the most important reproductive organ, which were carefully coded into indigestible structures and worked as the cornerstone of its health, reproduction, and development.

像拯救万物一般的基督；夏日感受，Luvluv，2022
而在接收到人类的观测之后，西瓜则失去了作为一个有机独立个体存在的权利、被迫承受来自人类社会的改造——剥夺原生性别，扼杀发展机遇，只能按照他者所期望和塑造的方向机械进化。

After receiving observation from the human race, watermelon loses its right to exist as an organic individual and is forced to undergo transformation by human society - depriving it of its native sex, stifling its opportunities for development, and evolving mechanically in the direction expected and shaped by “the other”.

外婆、妈妈、与我的共命运，徐艺函，2019
自此，在西瓜的水果哲学中，利他主义温和又暴力地抹平了政治化实践的可能，诗化般地、与女性主义的过往相吻合。正如波伏娃所说，女人不是天生的，而是后天形成的。

Since then, in the watermelon‘s philosophy of fruit, altruism smoothes out the possibility of politicized practices, gently and violently, and resonates the feminist history poetically. As Beauvoir said, one is not born, but rather becomes, a woman.

然而女性是一种处境，今日的西瓜亦然。巨型球体的内部，细小的种子均匀地散布着，由大量透红的果肉包裹，再被表层果皮所覆盖，如此共同构成一个有机的生命体。它的外表是单薄脆弱的， 内部是却柔软蓄力的，似乎有生生不息的力量，从这种后天重塑的、去中心化的生殖系统中源源不断地涌出、迸发，最终形成果实成熟那一刻时常听闻的爆破声——夏天来临的征兆。

Yet feminism is a situation, as is the watermelon of today. Inside the giant sphere, tiny seeds are evenly scattered, wrapped by a mass of translucent red flesh, and then covered by a superficial rind, thus together forming an organic life form. It is thin and fragile on the outside, but soft and powerful on the inside, and seems to have an endless stream of power that comes out of this decentralized reproductive system, which is remodeled after birth, and bursts out, culminating in the explosion sound that is often heard at the moment of fruit ripening - here comes the sign of summer.

Hardly More Than Ever, Laura Letinsky, 2002
那是一份掷地有声的反父权制中心话语体系宣言，是打破我们原来所认定的自身身份界限的第一枪，是游牧式、是游击战。西瓜的完全体意象，可以被视作所有二元对立关系在当代语境 下的转化方向——女权主义的探索、非二元性别的讨论、去殖民化的实践、反机构性垄断的当代艺术体系的建立……西瓜坚韧细腻，西瓜勇敢顽强；西瓜包容万象，西瓜百毒不侵。

It is a resounding declaration of the anti-patriarchal central discourse system; it is the first shot to break the boundaries of our original identity; it is the nomadic; it is guerrilla warfare. Watermelon's full-body imagery can be seen as the future direction of transformation of all dichotomies in the contemporary context - feminist exploration, non-binary gender discussion, decolonization practices, establishment of a contemporary art system against institutional monopoly, etc. Watermelon delicate and tough, watermelon tenacious and brave; watermelon tolerant of all things, watermelon invulnerable to all poisons.
