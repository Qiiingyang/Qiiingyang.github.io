童年时，记忆中的夏天总是从母亲手中递来的一角西瓜开始，因而在我个人历史的怀旧中，西瓜是夏天的窗户。

I always remember, during my childhood, that summer began with a dime of watermelon handed to me by my mother. Thus, in my personal history of nostalgia, watermelon is the window to summertime.

占位，小区居民，2022
